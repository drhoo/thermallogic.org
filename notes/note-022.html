Research Note 022 · 研究札记

Surface Maps and Hidden Hotspots表面图与隐藏热点。

The visible thermal field is a filtered signal of hidden heat structure.

可见热场是隐藏热结构经过过滤后的信号。

Surface thermography shows only what reaches the surface. Hidden hotspots are filtered by material depth, thermal resistance, spreading, and geometry before their signal becomes visible.

表面热成像只显示到达表面的内容。隐藏热点在变得可见之前，会被材料深度、热阻、扩散和几何结构过滤。

As depth increases, separate heat sources can blur into a single diffuse surface signature. The surface image is therefore not the source itself, but the source after passing through a thermal pathway.

随着深度增加，分离的热源可能在表面模糊成一个扩散区域。因此，表面图像不是热源本身，而是热源经过热路径后的信号。

Dynamic Thermal Control requires inverse thermal reading: using surface behaviour to infer the source, depth, pathway, and failure mode underneath.

动态热控制需要反向热读取：利用表面行为推断下方的源、深度、路径和失效模式。
