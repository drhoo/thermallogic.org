Research Note 007 · 研究札记

Resonant Thermal Coupling共振式热耦合。

Thermal energy can sometimes couple through preferred modes when source, carrier, medium, and receiver are suitably matched.

当源、载体、介质和接收体适当匹配时，热能有时可以通过偏好的模式进行耦合。

Resonance does not make heat magical, but it does show that energy transfer can become selective when timing, structure, and carrier match. A tuning fork accepts energy efficiently when the incoming disturbance matches its natural mode.

共振并不会让热变得神奇，但它说明当时序、结构和载体匹配时，能量转移可以变得有选择性。音叉会在来扰动与其自然模式匹配时高效接受能量。

Thermal systems also have carriers and modes: photons, phonons, polaritons, acoustic waves, fluid motions, phase states, and surface modes. Under suitable conditions, these can enhance, suppress, filter, bias, or redirect heat transfer.

热系统也有载体和模式：光子、声子、极化激元、声波、流体运动、相状态和表面模式。在适当条件下，它们可以增强、抑制、过滤、偏置或重新导向热转移。

The hard floor is scale and mechanism. Resonant thermal coupling is not room-scale magic. It depends on distance, material, geometry, frequency, boundary condition, temperature, and loss.

坚实底线是尺度和机制。共振式热耦合不是房间尺度的魔法。它取决于距离、材料、几何、频率、边界条件、温度和损耗。

The useful question is not whether heat can ignore the second law. It cannot. The useful question is whether thermal carriers can be made more addressable.

有用的问题不是热是否能无视热力学第二定律。不能。有用的问题是热载体是否可以变得更可寻址。
