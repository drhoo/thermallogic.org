Research Note 017 · 研究札记

Real-Time Thermal Prediction实时热预测。

Thermal control begins when hidden heat becomes visible enough to predict.

当隐藏的热变得足够可见以便预测时，热控制才真正开始。

Advanced packaging and 3D integrated circuits create heat fields that may not be visible from simple surface or point measurements. The dangerous thermal state can be internal, layered, and delayed.

先进封装和三维集成电路会产生无法通过简单表面或单点测量直接看见的热场。危险热状态可能是内部的、分层的、延迟显现的。

Real-time thermal prediction changes the control problem. The system no longer waits for a late alarm; it infers the hidden thermal field from partial measurements and physical constraints.

实时热预测改变了控制问题。系统不再等待滞后的警报，而是根据部分测量和物理约束推断隐藏热场。

This fits the Thermal Logic operating loop: sense, map, model, predict, act, and verify.

这符合热逻辑的运行循环：感知、绘制、建模、预测、行动和验证。
