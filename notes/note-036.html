Research Note 036 · 研究札记

Predictive Cooling and Pre-Emptive Flow Control预测式冷却与预先流量控制。

A thermal controller should not wait for overheating; it should predict the approaching thermal state and prepare the pathway in advance.

热控制器不应等待过热发生，而应预测即将到来的热状态，并提前准备路径。

Cooling should be anticipatory, not reactive.

Prediction allows the system to pre-cool, preserve margin, and avoid excessive pump energy by acting only when the future thermal state requires it.

The best cooling action may occur before the highest temperature is measured.
