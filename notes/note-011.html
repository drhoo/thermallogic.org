Research Note 011 · 研究札记

Planck: Limits, Reversibility, and Control限制、可逆性与控制。

Thermal control is strongest before useful difference is lost to irreversible dissipation.

热控制在有用差异尚未通过不可逆耗散丧失之前最有力量。

Planck is valuable because he gives Thermal Logic its hard floor. He does not begin with clever mechanisms, but with states, changes of state, equilibrium, energy, entropy, and reversibility.

普朗克的重要性在于，他为热逻辑提供了坚实底线。他不是从巧妙装置开始，而是从状态、状态变化、平衡、能量、熵和可逆性开始。

No control system is allowed to outrun thermodynamics. We may redirect, delay, store, spread, concentrate, pump, convert, and shed heat, but we cannot make heat disappear or recover all dissipated availability.

任何控制系统都不能超越热力学。我们可以重新导向、延迟、储存、扩散、集中、泵送、转换和散出热，但不能让热消失，也不能完全恢复已经耗散的可用性。

The key control window is early. Once useful difference is flattened into low-grade uniform warmth, the control opportunity has largely passed.

关键控制窗口在早期。一旦有用差异被拉平为低品位的均匀温热，控制机会大多已经过去。

The aim is not to defeat thermodynamics, but to act earlier within it.

目标不是战胜热力学，而是在热力学之内更早行动。
