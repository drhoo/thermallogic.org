Research Note 024 · 研究札记

Uncertainty-Aware Thermal Models不确定性感知热模型。

A thermal model must know when it does not know.

热模型必须知道自己什么时候不知道。

As thermal systems become model-driven, prediction alone is not enough. A control model must also estimate whether the input lies inside its learned validity domain.

随着热系统变得由模型驱动，仅有预测是不够的。控制模型还必须估计输入是否位于其学习过的有效域内。

Uncertainty-aware learning uses model uncertainty as a signal of familiarity. Low uncertainty suggests the model is operating in a region similar to its training experience; high uncertainty warns that the model may be extrapolating.

不确定性感知学习使用模型不确定性作为熟悉度信号。低不确定性说明模型处于类似训练经验的区域；高不确定性则警告模型可能正在外推。

For Dynamic Thermal Control, this is a safety principle: prediction should travel with confidence, and low-confidence states should trigger conservative control or further sensing.

对动态热控制而言，这是一个安全原则：预测应伴随置信度；低置信状态应触发保守控制或进一步传感。
