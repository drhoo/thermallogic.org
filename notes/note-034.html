Thermal Stability Thresholds热稳定阈值。

A thermal system can appear stable until a threshold is crossed, after which heat generation and temperature rise reinforce each other.

热系统可以看似稳定，直到跨过某个阈值；之后产热和温升会相互强化。

Thermal runaway is not only an event; it has a stability threshold.

热失控不仅是一个事件，它还有稳定性阈值。

Small changes in geometry, material properties, spacing, or heat generation can move a system closer to or further from instability.

几何、材料属性、间距或产热的小变化，都可能使系统更接近或远离不稳定。

Thermal control must identify how close the system is to instability, not merely whether it is hot right now.

热控制必须判断系统距离不稳定还有多近，而不只是判断当前是否很热。
