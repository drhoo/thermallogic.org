Research Note 001 · 研究札记

Poynting & Thomson: Heat as Exchange热作为交换。

Equilibrium is not stillness. It is balanced exchange.

平衡不是静止，而是被平衡的交换。

Poynting and Thomson invite us to treat heat not as a static quantity but as an exchange process. A body at equilibrium is not necessarily inactive. It may be receiving and giving energy in balanced measure.

对热逻辑而言，平衡并不意味着没有发生任何事情。它可能意味着进入与离开的能量正在相互抵消。

For Thermal Logic™, this matters because control begins when exchange becomes visible. If energy is entering, leaving, storing, delaying, or converting, then the system has behaviour that can be mapped.

只要交换存在，就可能存在路径、时序、储存、延迟和控制。

Heat is therefore not only a number on a thermometer. It is a relationship: between source and sink, surface and medium, timing and pathway.

热不只是温度计上的数字。它也是源与汇、表面与介质、时序与路径之间的关系。
