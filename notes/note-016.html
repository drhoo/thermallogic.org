Research Note 016 · 研究札记

Data Centres: From Chip to Facility从芯片到设施的数据中心热路径。

High-density data centres expose heat as a linked system, not a local nuisance: chip flux, package resistance, branch flow, rack density, facility rejection, and timing are one connected thermal path.

高密度数据中心揭示了热并非局部麻烦，而是一个连接系统：芯片热通量、封装热阻、分支流量、机架密度、设施排热和时序共同构成一条热路径。

High-density data centres are not merely hot rooms. They are linked thermal systems where local chip heat flux, package resistance, rack density, coolant flow, power availability, and facility rejection interact.

高密度数据中心并不只是炎热的房间。它们是相互连接的热系统，其中局部芯片热通量、封装热阻、机架密度、冷却液流量、电力供应和设施排热相互作用。

The phrase “from chip to facility” matters because the thermal problem does not stop at the cold plate. Heat moves through a chain, and every link in the chain can create resistance, delay, loss, or failure.

“从芯片到设施”这个表述很重要，因为热问题并不会在冷板处结束。热通过一条链条移动，链条中的每一环都可能产生热阻、延迟、损失或失效。

Dynamic Thermal Control™ begins when the chip-to-facility heat path becomes readable, addressable, and adjustable.

当从芯片到设施的热路径变得可读、可寻址和可调节时，动态热控制就开始了。
