Research Note 015 · 研究札记

Incropera: Heat Transfer as Design Network传热作为设计网络。

A thermal system is a network of rates, resistances, surfaces, flows, and boundaries — and any network can be mapped for control.

热系统是由速率、热阻、表面、流动和边界组成的网络；任何网络都可以被绘制并用于控制。

Modern heat-transfer engineering gives Thermal Logic a crucial component: thermal resistance. Heat does not merely spread. It moves through a resistance network.

现代传热工程为热逻辑提供了一个关键组件：热阻。热并不只是扩散，它通过热阻网络移动。

In a data centre, that network may run from chip hotspot to silicon, package, thermal interface material, cold plate, coolant boundary layer, branch flow, heat exchanger, and facility rejection.

在数据中心中，这个网络可能从芯片热点延伸到硅片、封装、导热界面材料、冷板、冷却液边界层、分支流量、换热器和设施级排热。

A hotspot may therefore be not simply too much heat, but one resistance in the chain being too high for the local heat rate.

因此，热点可能并不只是热太多，而是链条中的某个热阻相对于局部热流率过高。

Dynamic Thermal Control begins when heat-transfer modelling moves from design-time calculation to live operating control.

当传热建模从设计时计算转变为实时运行控制时，动态热控制就开始了。
