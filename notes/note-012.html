Research Note 012 · 研究札记

Partington: Free Energy, Surface, and State自由能、表面与状态。

Thermal control is not only temperature control. It is state control: energy, surface, phase, material, and equilibrium.

热控制不只是温度控制，而是状态控制：能量、表面、相态、材料和平衡。

Partington reminds us that temperature is not the whole story. A system’s thermal behaviour can involve free energy, phase, pressure, surface energy, adsorption, chemistry, and equilibrium.

Partington 提醒我们，温度并不是全部。系统的热行为可能涉及自由能、相态、压力、表面能、吸附、化学和平衡。

For Dynamic Thermal Control™, this matters because a coolant path is not just a pipe. It is a controlled thermodynamic interface: fluid, wall, surface, pressure, phase margin, dissolved gases, and material compatibility all matter.

对动态热控制而言，这很重要，因为冷却液路径不只是管道。它是一个受控的热力学界面：流体、管壁、表面、压力、相态余量、溶解气体和材料兼容性都很重要。

Free energy sharpens the idea of useful difference. The real loss is not energy destroyed, but controllable usefulness lost through dissipation.

自由能让有用差异的概念更加清晰。真正的损失不是能量被毁灭，而是通过耗散丧失了可控制的有用性。

A coolant path is not just a pipe. It is a controlled thermodynamic interface.

冷却液路径不只是管道。它是一个受控的热力学界面。
