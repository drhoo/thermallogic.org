Research Note 025 · 研究札记

Predictive Thermal Operating Loop预测式热操作循环。

Dynamic Thermal Control is the live control of thermal networks using sensing, modelling, prediction, action, and verification.

动态热控制是利用感知、建模、预测、行动和验证对热网络进行实时控制。

The recent papers converge on a clear operating pattern. Heat must be sensed as a field, hidden hotspots must be inferred, and thermal pathways must be treated as networks of resistance, flow, pressure, phase, and boundary condition.

近期论文汇聚成一种清晰的运行模式。热必须作为场来感知，隐藏热点必须被推断，热路径必须被视为由热阻、流量、压力、相态和边界条件组成的网络。

Control then acts before useful difference is lost: adjust coolant flow, pressure, phase boundary, load, pathway, or buffer depending on the identified failure mode.

随后控制必须在有用差异丧失之前行动：根据识别出的失效模式，调节冷却液流量、压力、相变边界、负载、路径或缓冲。

Sense → map → infer → predict → act → verify. That is the first practical Thermal Logic operating loop.

感知 → 绘制 → 推断 → 预测 → 行动 → 验证。这是第一个实用的热逻辑操作循环。
