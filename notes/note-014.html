Research Note 014 · 研究札记

Beretta & Gyftopoulos: Heat as Interaction热作为相互作用。

Heat is not stored thermal stuff. It is an interaction: energy transfer accompanied by entropy transfer, distinguishable from work.

热不是被储存的热物质，而是一种相互作用：伴随熵转移的能量转移，并且不同于功。

This modern reference sharpens our language. Heat is not a substance stored inside a body. Work and heat are modes of transfer. A system stores internal energy, not heat.

这个现代参考文献让我们的语言更加精确。热不是储存在物体中的物质。功和热是转移方式。系统储存的是内能，而不是热。

The crucial distinction is entropy. Work transfers energy without entropy transfer. Heat transfers energy with entropy transfer under the relevant thermodynamic conditions.

关键区别在于熵。功转移能量而不转移熵。热在相关热力学条件下转移能量并伴随熵转移。

For Dynamic Thermal Control, this means the aim is not merely to move thermal energy away from a hotspot. The aim is to move it with minimum unnecessary entropy generation across the full path.

对动态热控制而言，这意味着目标不只是把热能从热点移走，而是在完整路径中以最少不必要熵产生来移动它。

To control heat rigorously, track not only energy movement but where entropy is transferred or generated.

要严格控制热，不仅要追踪能量运动，还要追踪熵在哪里被转移或产生。
