Research Note 013 · 研究札记

Schroeder: Entropy, Probability, and Control熵、概率与控制。

Thermal behaviour is not random noise. It is statistical tendency, and statistical tendency can be measured, predicted, and sometimes guided.

热行为不是随机噪声，而是统计趋势；统计趋势可以被测量、预测，有时也可以被引导。

Schroeder gives Thermal Logic a modern teaching bridge between thermodynamics and statistical mechanics. Heat cannot be understood only from the outside; bulk temperature is the visible result of enormous microscopic populations.

Schroeder 为热逻辑提供了热力学与统计力学之间的现代教学桥梁。热不能只从外部理解；整体温度是巨大微观群体的可见结果。

Entropy becomes less mysterious when seen statistically. Energy spreads because there are overwhelmingly more ways for it to be distributed than contained.

从统计角度看，熵就不那么神秘。能量会扩散，是因为它被分布开的方式远远多于被局部保留的方式。

Dynamic Thermal Control does not control every particle. It controls pathways, boundaries, flow, pressure, surface, phase, and timing — the conditions that make better thermal outcomes more likely.

动态热控制并不是控制每一个粒子。它控制路径、边界、流量、压力、表面、相态和时序——也就是让更好热结果更可能发生的条件。

Control the conditions, and the statistical outcome may change.

控制条件，统计结果就可能改变。
