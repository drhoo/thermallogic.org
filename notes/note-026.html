Research Note 026 · 研究札记

Geometric Flow Logic几何流动逻辑。

Cooling performance can be programmed into geometry: pore size, unit scale, porosity gradient, and flow direction shape how coolant reaches and protects the surface.

冷却性能可以被写入几何结构：孔隙尺寸、单元尺度、孔隙率梯度和流动方向共同塑造冷却剂如何到达并保护表面。

A cooling surface is not merely a surface. It can be a flow-distribution logic layer.

Triply Periodic Minimal Surface structures show how porosity, unit size, and gradient direction can shape coolant distribution before any active control is added.

The best thermal pathway is not always the most open path, but the one that keeps the carrier attached, distributed, and useful.
