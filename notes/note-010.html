Research Note 010 · 研究札记

Preston: Heat as Connected System热作为连接系统。

Heat cannot be understood by isolated facts. It must be mapped through relations: measurement, pathway, surface, availability, and state.

热不能靠孤立事实来理解，而必须通过测量、路径、表面、可用性和状态之间的关系来绘制。

Preston’s Theory of Heat is useful because it treats heat as a connected system rather than a pile of separate facts. The value is not merely in knowing more effects, but in seeing their relations.

Preston 的《热理论》有用，因为它把热视为一个连接系统，而不是一堆分散事实。价值不只是知道更多效应，而是看见它们之间的关系。

A model is useful only while it remains accountable to measurement. Theory must remain chained to experiment, correction, and comparison.

模型只有在对测量负责时才有用。理论必须始终与实验、校正和比较相连。

Preston strengthens one of Thermal Logic’s central principles: dissipation is not the destruction of energy. It is the loss of usable difference.

Preston 强化了热逻辑的核心原则之一：耗散不是能量的毁灭，而是可用差异的丧失。

Dynamic Thermal Control does not try to destroy heat. It tries to preserve, redirect, delay, spread, shed, or convert useful difference before it becomes low-grade sameness.

动态热控制并不是试图消灭热，而是在有用差异变成低品位均匀状态之前，保存、重新导向、延迟、扩散、散出或转换它。
