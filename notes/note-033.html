Research Note 033 · 研究札记

Coupled-Field Thermal Failure耦合场热失效。

Thermal failure is not governed by temperature alone; it emerges from coupled temperature, stress, electrical behaviour, material properties, and geometry.

热失效不只由温度支配，而是由温度、应力、电行为、材料性质和几何结构的耦合产生。

Highest temperature is not always highest failure risk.

最高温并不总是最高失效风险。

Failure can emerge where heat, tensile stress, carrier behaviour, bandgap shift, and thermal conductivity degradation reinforce one another.

失效可能出现在热、拉伸应力、载流子行为、带隙变化和导热率退化相互强化的位置。

A thermal operating system must measure or infer the coupled state, not just the temperature state.

热操作系统必须测量或推断耦合状态，而不只是温度状态。
