Research Note 008 · 研究札记

Maxwell: Dynamic Stability动态稳定。

A system can be stable because it is moving correctly, not because it is still.

一个系统之所以稳定，可能不是因为它静止，而是因为它以正确的方式运动。

Maxwell’s dynamical top is not about heat directly. It is about stability under motion. A top standing still falls, but a spinning top can remain dynamically stable because stored motion changes its response to disturbance.

麦克斯韦的动力陀螺并不是直接关于热，而是关于运动中的稳定。静止的陀螺会倒下，而旋转的陀螺可以因为储存的运动而保持动态稳定。

For Thermal Logic, this matters because thermal stability may also be active rather than static. A system can contain continuous flow, storage, delay, and release while remaining stable.

对热逻辑而言，这很重要，因为热稳定也可能是主动的，而不是静态的。一个系统可以包含持续流动、储存、延迟和释放，同时仍保持稳定。

The response of a system is not determined only by the disturbance applied. It is shaped by internal distribution, stored energy, pathways, boundaries, friction, and timing.

系统的响应并不只由外加扰动决定。它还由内部分布、储存能量、路径、边界、摩擦和时序塑造。

Thermal stability may not mean removing all heat. It may mean arranging flow, storage, delay, and release so the system remains dynamically balanced.

热稳定不一定意味着移除所有热。它可能意味着安排流动、储存、延迟和释放，使系统保持动态平衡。
