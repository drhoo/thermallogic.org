Research Note 009 · 研究札记

Glazebrook: Heat as Measured Behaviour热作为被测量的行为。

A thermal system becomes controllable only when its transfers can be measured, mapped, corrected, and compared.

只有当热的转移能够被测量、绘制、校正和比较时，热系统才可能变得可控制。

Glazebrook treats heat as something learned through practical experiment. Heat is not only a theory. It is behaviour made visible by apparatus, readings, correction, and comparison.

Glazebrook 将热视为通过实际实验来理解的对象。热不只是理论，而是由仪器、读数、校正和比较显现出来的行为。

His laboratory method is important for Thermal Logic because a control system cannot be built from impressions. It must distinguish temperature, transfer, loss, pathway, surface, and measurement error.

他的实验方法对热逻辑很重要，因为控制系统不能建立在印象之上。它必须区分温度、转移、损耗、路径、表面和测量误差。

A single point reading is weak. A curve is stronger. A field map is stronger still. Dynamic Thermal Control requires the movement from point sensing toward mapped thermal behaviour.

单点读数很弱。曲线更强。场图更强。动态热控制需要从点测量走向热行为地图。

Sensing is not passive. The geometry and method of the instrument select the thermal reality being reported.

测量不是被动的。仪器的几何和方法会选择它所报告的热现实。
