Research Note 005 · 研究札记

C. V. Boys: Surface as Instrument表面作为仪器。

A boundary is not where behaviour ends. It is where behaviour becomes visible.

边界不是行为结束的地方，而是行为变得可见的地方。

C. V. Boys showed how simple-looking surfaces can reveal precise physical behaviour. A soap film is not merely a fragile skin. It is a surface under tension, a changing thickness, an optical indicator, and a stability problem at once.

C. V. Boys 展示了看似简单的表面如何揭示精确的物理行为。肥皂膜不只是脆弱的薄皮，它同时是受张力的表面、变化的厚度、光学指示器和稳定性问题。

As a film drains and thins, colour becomes a measure of thickness. A visible surface becomes a readable record of invisible state.

当薄膜排液并变薄时，颜色成为厚度的测量方式。可见的表面成为隐藏状态的可读记录。

For Thermal Logic, this is a major principle. Surfaces are not passive endings. They govern contact, radiation, wetting, phase change, boundary layers, thermal resistance, and exchange.

对热逻辑而言，这是一个重要原则。表面不是被动的终点。它们支配接触、辐射、润湿、相变、边界层、热阻和交换。

The boundary is not merely the edge of the system. The boundary is a component.

边界不只是系统的边缘。边界本身就是一个组件。
