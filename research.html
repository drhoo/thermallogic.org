Research Notes

Foundations for Thermal Logic

Short reflections connecting historical physics, modern thermal control, and the emerging component language of Dynamic Thermal Control.

这些研究札记连接历史物理、现代热控制，以及动态热控制所需要的基础语言。

Research Note 001

Poynting & Thomson: Heat as Exchange

Equilibrium is not stillness. It is balanced exchange.

平衡不是静止，而是被平衡的交换。

Research Note 002

Maxwell: Heat, Motion, and Selection

Disorder may not be the end of control. It may be the point where better information becomes necessary.

无序未必是控制的终点，它可能是需要更好信息的起点。

Research Note 003

Fleming: Waves, Media, and Boundaries

Disturbance becomes controllable when its medium, timing, and boundaries are understood.

当介质、时序和边界被理解时，扰动就可能被控制。
