Research Note 026 — Geometric Flow LogicTPMS transpiration cooling shows that passive geometry can distribute coolant, improve uniformity, and prevent wasteful detachment when designed with the right gradients.TPMS 渗透冷却显示，被动几何结构可以分配冷却剂、改善均匀性，并通过合适梯度避免低效脱离。
Research Note 027 — Conductivity as System StateMetal hydride bed modelling shows that thermal conductivity can evolve during operation as particle contact, pore gas, pressure, temperature, and reaction fraction change.金属氢化物床模型显示，随着颗粒接触、孔隙气体、压力、温度和反应比例变化，导热率会在运行中演化。
Research Note 028 — Thermal Rectification and Phonon DirectionalityCarbon/boron nitride heteronanotube research shows that asymmetric phonon transport can create thermal diode behaviour at the nanoscale.碳/氮化硼异质纳米管研究显示，非对称声子传输可以在纳米尺度产生热二极管行为。
Research Note 029 — Strain, Interfaces, and Hotspot PathwaysStrained AlGaN/GaN FinFET modelling shows that better conduction inside one layer may not help if interface transport becomes the bottleneck.受应变 AlGaN/GaN FinFET 建模显示，如果界面传输成为瓶颈，某一层内部导热改善也可能无济于事。
Research Note 030 — Thermal Runaway and Propagation PathwaysLithium-ion battery module studies show that thermal runaway propagation depends on where it begins, which pathways are available, and how much stored energy is present.锂离子电池模块研究显示，热失控传播取决于起始位置、可用路径和储存能量水平。
Research Note 031 — Phase Change as Thermal TimingBattery PCM reviews show that phase change is useful as a thermal capacitor, but only when coupled to a pathway that can drain and reset it.电池 PCM 综述显示，相变作为热电容很有用，但前提是它连接到可以排热并复位的路径。
Research Note 032 — Thermal Cloaking and Field PreservationTransient thermal cloak research shows that dynamic heat control must consider not only conductivity, but heat capacity, timing, and field evolution.瞬态热隐身研究显示，动态热控制不仅要考虑导热率，还要考虑热容、时序和场演化。
Research Note 033 — Coupled-Field Thermal FailureIntegrated Raman–PL semiconductor analysis shows that the most dangerous point may be where heat, stress, and material response reinforce one another, not simply the hottest point.集成 Raman–PL 半导体分析显示，最危险的位置可能不是最高温点，而是热、应力和材料响应相互强化的位置。
Research Note 034 — Thermal Stability ThresholdsAnalytical battery-pack stability models show that geometry, material properties, heat generation feedback, and dissipation pathways determine the transition from stable to unstable behaviour.电池组稳定性解析模型显示，几何、材料属性、产热反馈和散热路径决定系统从稳定到不稳定的转变。
Research Note 035 — Thermal Diodes and One-Way Heat PathsTopology-optimized vapor-channel thermal diodes show that geometry can create passive one-way heat behaviour useful for isolating overheating and preventing backflow.拓扑优化蒸汽通道热二极管显示，几何结构可以创造被动单向导热行为，用于隔离过热并防止热回流。
Research Note 036 — Predictive Cooling and Pre-Emptive Flow ControlLSTM–MPC liquid-cooling research shows that forecast-driven flow control can reduce over-temperature duration and pump energy by acting before the thermal peak arrives.LSTM–MPC 液冷研究显示，由预测驱动的流量控制可以在热峰到来之前行动，从而减少超温时间和泵能耗。
