EN / 中文 Short foundation notes for Thermal Logic.

Foundation notes.基础札记。

Short technical reflections connecting historical physics, modern thermal control, and the emerging component language of Dynamic Thermal Control™.

这些研究札记连接历史物理、现代热控制，以及动态热控制所需要的基础语言。

Research Note 001 — Poynting & Thomson: Heat as ExchangeHeat is not merely a static quantity. It is a relation of exchange between source, sink, surface, medium, timing, and pathway.热不只是静态数量，而是源、汇、表面、介质、时序和路径之间的交换关系。
Research Note 002 — Maxwell: Heat, Motion, and SelectionThermal disorder may contain distinctions of speed, direction, timing, boundary, and carrier that are lost when we look only at bulk temperature.热无序中可能仍包含速度、方向、时序、边界和载体等差异，而这些在只看整体温度时会被忽略。
Research Note 003 — Fleming: Waves, Media, and BoundariesA disturbance is shaped by the medium it crosses, the timing of its motion, and the boundaries that reflect, absorb, delay, or transform it.扰动会被其经过的介质、运动时序以及反射、吸收、延迟或转化它的边界所塑造。
Research Note 004 — Maxwell: Flow, Accumulation, and DissipationA hotspot is not merely a high temperature. It is a local imbalance between heat entering, spreading, storing, and escaping.热点不只是高温，而是热的进入、扩散、储存和逸出之间的局部失衡。
Research Note 005 — C. V. Boys: Surface as InstrumentSoap films, thin layers, surface tension, colour, drainage, and rupture show that surfaces can measure, reveal, and govern hidden physical states.肥皂膜、薄层、表面张力、颜色、排液和破裂说明表面能够测量、揭示并支配隐藏的物理状态。
Research Note 006 — Maxwell: Scientific Metaphor and Thermal MethodMaxwell’s method gives Thermal Logic permission to use analogies only where the formal relation survives.麦克斯韦的方法允许热逻辑使用类比，但前提是其形式关系仍然成立。
Research Note 007 — Resonant Thermal CouplingHeat does not always spread as featureless diffusion. Radiation, phonons, acoustic modes, surfaces, and structures can create selective coupling under strict conditions.热并不总是无特征地扩散。辐射、声子、声学模式、表面和结构在严格条件下可以形成选择性耦合。
Research Note 008 — Maxwell: Dynamic StabilityMaxwell’s dynamical top shows that stability can arise from stored motion, distribution, axis, constraint, friction, and timing.麦克斯韦的陀螺说明，稳定性可以来自储存的运动、分布、轴线、约束、摩擦和时序。
Research Note 009 — Glazebrook: Heat as Measured BehaviourGlazebrook gives Thermal Logic a laboratory spine: simple apparatus, practical experiment, correction of error, thermal curves, radiation sensing, and pathway awareness.Glazebrook 为热逻辑提供了实验骨架：简单仪器、实际实验、误差校正、热曲线、辐射测量和路径意识。
Research Note 010 — Preston: Heat as Connected SystemPreston gives Thermal Logic the broad architecture: experiment, theory, radiation, material selectivity, entropy, availability, correction, and thermal state.Preston 为热逻辑提供了广阔架构：实验、理论、辐射、材料选择性、熵、可用性、校正和热状态。
Research Note 011 — Planck: Limits, Reversibility, and ControlPlanck gives Thermal Logic the hard floor: reversibility, irreversibility, entropy, limits, and the need to judge the whole path, not only the hotspot.普朗克为热逻辑提供了坚实底线：可逆性、不可逆性、熵、限制，以及必须评价完整路径而不只是热点。
Research Note 012 — Partington: Free Energy, Surface, and StatePartington brings free energy, stable equilibrium, surface thermodynamics, phase behaviour, chemistry, and material state into Thermal Logic.Partington 将自由能、稳定平衡、表面热力学、相变行为、化学和材料状态带入热逻辑。
Research Note 013 — Schroeder: Entropy, Probability, and ControlSchroeder brings the modern statistical view: entropy, multiplicity, transport, free energy, and the link between microscopic population and bulk behaviour.Schroeder 带来现代统计视角：熵、多重性、传输、自由能，以及微观群体与宏观行为之间的联系。
Research Note 014 — Beretta & Gyftopoulos: Heat as InteractionBeretta and Gyftopoulos give Thermal Logic a modern language correction: heat is not stored; internal energy is stored. Heat and work are modes of transfer.Beretta 与 Gyftopoulos 为热逻辑提供了现代语言校正：热不是被储存的；被储存的是内能。热和功都是转移方式。
Research Note 015 — Incropera: Heat Transfer as Design NetworkIncropera gives Thermal Logic the engineering bridge: heat-transfer rates, thermal resistance networks, boundary conditions, heat exchangers, micro/nanoscale effects, and computational modelling.Incropera 为热逻辑提供了工程桥梁：传热速率、热阻网络、边界条件、换热器、微纳尺度效应和计算建模。
